Global Logistics for Marine Spares

Universal Exports BVN is not just a stockist; we are a logistics partner for ship managers worldwide. Located in Bhavnagar, just 50km from the Alang Ship Breaking Yard, we have immediate access to high-quality machinery from decommissioned vessels.

Our inventory includes critical spares for Main Engines (MAN B&W, Wartsila, Sulzer), Auxiliary Engines (Yanmar, Daihatsu), and Purifiers (Alfa Laval, Westfalia). Every item listed above is physically present in our warehouse, ready for inspection and dispatch. We offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) services to major maritime hubs including Dubai, Singapore, Rotterdam, and Houston.
