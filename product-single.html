This ABB VTR 564-11 turbocharger was recovered from a 2012-built Capesize Bulk Carrier during recycling at Alang Yard Plot #XX. The vessel arrived under its own power, and the turbocharger was inspected in operation prior to beaching. The logbook indicated regular maintenance intervals by ABB service engineers.

Upon arrival at our workshop, the unit was completely dismantled. The rotor shaft was checked for run-out (within 0.02mm). The turbine blades were cleaned using walnut shell blasting and inspected for foreign object damage (FOD). No cracks were found during Dye Penetrant testing.

The unit has been reassembled with a complete new bearing kit (Part No. 54100) and new oil pumps. It is currently stored in a climate-controlled environment, filled with preservation oil.
