Crankcase Protection

Preventing Crankcase Explosions

Oil Mist Detectors (OMD) are the first line of defense against catastrophic crankcase explosions. By detecting the opacity of oil mist generated by overheating bearings or piston rings, these systems trigger alarms or shutdowns before the mist reaches explosive concentrations.

At Universal Exports, we understand that a faulty OMD is a major safety non-compliance issue. We stock complete reconditioned systems, replacement detector heads, and control units for the most common marine OMD models.

Our Testing Process

  • 1. Optics Cleaning: The most common cause of failure is dirty optics. We ultrasonically clean all lenses and mirrors to ensure accurate light transmission.
  • 2. Electronic Verification: Control boards are tested for signal processing accuracy. We verify that the alarm logic triggers at the correct opacity thresholds.
  • 3. Fan & Airflow Check: For systems like Graviner Mk6, the extraction fan is critical. We test motor current and airflow rates.
  • 4. Sensitivity Calibration: Using standard calibration filters, we verify the detector's sensitivity to ensure it meets class requirements.
