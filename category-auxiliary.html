Reliable Power

Low-Hour GenSets from Premium Vessels

The auxiliary engine is the heartbeat of a vessel's electrical system. At Universal Exports, we understand that reliability is paramount. We do not deal in "scrap" engines. Our procurement team specifically targets vessels with well-maintained machinery records, often sourcing engines with less than 20,000 running hours.

We stock both complete Generator Sets (Engine + Alternator on skid) and bare engines. Whether you need a Yanmar 6N18L for a tugboat or a Daihatsu 6DK-20 for a bulk carrier, we have the inventory and the technical expertise to deliver a plug-and-play solution.

Our Reconditioning & Testing Standards

  • 1. Crankshaft Inspection: We perform MPI (Magnetic Particle Inspection) on all journals to check for micro-cracks. Hardness and surface finish are verified to be within OEM limits.
  • 2. Cylinder Head Overhaul: All heads are pressure tested. Valves and seats are ground or replaced. Rotocaps are checked for proper rotation.
  • 3. Piston & Liner Calibration: Pistons are cleaned and ring grooves are measured. Liners are honed to restore the cross-hatch pattern for optimal lubrication.
  • 4. Fuel Injection System: Fuel pumps are calibrated on our test bench. Injectors are pressure tested for spray pattern and atomization.
  • 5. Alternator Care: The alternator is cleaned, varnished, and baked. Bearings are replaced. Megger tests and PI (Polarization Index) tests are conducted.
  • 6. Load Bank Testing: Upon request, we can arrange for a load bank test to demonstrate the engine's performance under 50%, 75%, and 100% load.
